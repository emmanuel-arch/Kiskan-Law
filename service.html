Bemih Kanyonge

Managing Partner, Advocate

Biography

Bemih is an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya. He has experience working in civil society, private practice and public sector. He has extensive experience in Land transactions, land dispute and construction disputes resolution, commercial and tax litigation, civil and criminal litigation and has been involved in public interest litigation. He has also appeared before tribunals most notably The Sports Tribunal, The Tax Appeals Tribunal, the Business Premises Tribunal etc.

He also has experience in commercial transactions. He is has previously consulted for and worked with Trade Mark East Africa (TMEA), State Department for East African Affairs, Amnesty International, Transparency International-Kenya, Legal Resources Foundation, East Africa Centre for Human Rights, Nairobi City County, Muthaura Mugambi Ayugi & Njonjo (MMAN) Advocates, Kinuthia Kahindi & Co. Advocates, Kituo cha Sheria and The Open Society Foundations, Budapest, Hungary – an international non-governmental organisation. Bemih has over 13 years work experience.
